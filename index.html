Our Amazing Ulifa Lodge Experience

Our Luxurious Spacious Rooms

Our lodges are designed with an eye for luxury, featuring well-appointed rooms, modern amenities, and elegant decor that reflects the essence of comfort.

All rooms are fully Air-Conditioned with Smart TV’s, Complimentary Tea and Coffee, Hotel Quality Linen, a Work Station, Bathroom En-Suite, Daily Room Servicing with Unlimited Wi-Fi, Secure Parking with 24 hour-CCTV cameras.



Versatile Venues

Whether it's a grand wedding, a corporate conference, or an intimate celebration, our venues are tailored to accommodate a range of events, each infused with the distinctive Ulifa touch.

Spa Retreats

Relax and rejuvenate at our spas, where skilled therapists offer a range of treatments to soothe the mind, body, and soul.

Comfortable Accommodations

All our 19 rooms are fully air-conditioned, equipped with smart TVs, complimentary tea and coffee, hotel-quality linen, workstations, and en-suite bathrooms. Enjoy the convenience of daily room servicing and unlimited Wi-Fi.

Secure and Sustainable

Your safety is our priority. Benefit from secure parking, 24-hour CCTV cameras, and a dedicated security guard. We've also implemented sustainable practices with solar systems, generators, and water tankers for uninterrupted services during load shedding or water shortages.

Full English Breakfast

Start your day with a full English breakfast served in our dining hall. Lunch and dinner are available on request, promising a delightful culinary experience.
