ABOUT US

Discover Our Underground

Welcome to Ulifa Lodge & Wedding Venue, an exquisite 3-Star retreat nestled in the heart of Westville, Durban, under the Ethekwini municipality. Established in 2019 by the visionary Dumisani Madi, Ulifa Investment Pty Ltd proudly operates this haven designed to provide an unparalleled lodging experience to guests from all corners of South Africa.

Beyond being a luxurious lodge, Ulifa boasts a versatile venue that caters not only to the needs of corporate events but also to those seeking the perfect backdrop for their special day. Our venue is equipped to accommodate conferences with a capacity of over 30 people, ensuring a seamless and productive environment for your business needs.

For those dreaming of an enchanting outdoor event, our sanctuary garden sets the stage for unforgettable moments. Whether it's a picturesque picnic or a fairy-tale garden wedding, Ulifa Lodge & Wedding Venue offers the perfect setting to turn your dreams into reality.
