Property Description

Escape to Ulifa Lodge and Functions Westville, an extension of our legacy, established in 2023. Located in Westville, just 10 km from Durban beachfront and 40 km from King Shaka International Airport, our lodge offers a serene retreat with 15 well-appointed rooms.
