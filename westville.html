Property Description

Escape to Ulifa Lodge and Functions, an extension of our legacy, established in 2023. Located in Westville, just 10 km from Durban beachfront and 40 km from King Shaka International Airport, our lodge offers a serene retreat with 15 well-appointed rooms.


Experience the perfect balance of tranquility and convenience at Ulifa Lodge & Functions. Whether you're exploring Durban or attending a business engagement, our lodge welcomes you to a haven of comfort and hospitality. We look forward to making your stay memorable.
